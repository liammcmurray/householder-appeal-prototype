Appeal a householder planning decision

Who can use this service?

Use this service if you want to appeal a householder planning decision.

For other types of appeal, use the appeal a planning decision service.

Start your appeal

You'll need certain documents to complete your appeal:

  • the decision letter from your local planning department
  • your appeal statement

You'll also need your original planning application form, which you can search for on your local planning department's website.​

​

Your appeal statement

You’ll need to write an appeal statement that sets out why you think the local planning department's decision is incorrect. This should respond to all of the reasons for refusal (listed on the decision letter) and include any other information that supports your appeal.

How to appeal

Applying online will speed up the appeals process.

If you cannot use the online service, contact the Planning Inspectorate customer support team on 0303 444 5000, or email:
enquiries@planninginspectorate.gov.uk
