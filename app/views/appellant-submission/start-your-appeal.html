Appeal a householder planning decision

Start your appeal

You'll need certain documents to complete your appeal:

  • the decision letter from your local planning department
  • your appeal statement
  • your planning application form

If you do not have a copy of your planning application form, you can search for it on your local planning department's website.

​

Your appeal statement

This is the only opportunity to appeal your planning decision. ​

You’ll need to write and upload a statement during the appeal process that explains why you think the local planning department's decision is incorrect. You'll need to respond to all the reasons for refusal (listed on the decision letter), and include any other information that supports your appeal.

How to appeal

Applying online will speed up the appeals process.

If you cannot use the online service, contact the Planning Inspectorate customer support team on 0303 444 5000, or email:
enquiries@planninginspectorate.gov.uk

Return to a saved appeal​

You will have been sent an email with a link to continue an appeal that has been started.​
