The status of your planning appeal

We’ve received your appeal.

We need to make sure your appeal is valid, which means checking it includes all the necessary documents.

​This should take around 2 weeks but may take longer in busy periods.

What happens next?

  • Once we confirm we have everything we need, we'll start your appeal. 
  • Your local planning department will then submit their evidence. This usually takes around 1 week after you submit your appeal. ​
  • An Inspector may need to make a site visit. ​
  • The Inspector will then consider all the evidence to make their decision.
