What happens next?​

We need to make sure your appeal is valid. This should take around 2 weeks, but may take longer in busy periods.​

  • Once we’ve checked that your appeal is valid, we’ll start your appeal. ​
  • Your local planning department will then submit their evidence. This usually takes around 1 week after you submit your appeal. ​
  • An Inspector may need to make a site visit. ​

We will contact you at each of these stages.​
